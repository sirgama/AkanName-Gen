AKAN NAMES

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

MALE NAMES
  • Sunday: Kwasi
    • Monday: Kwadwo
    • Tuesday: Kwabena
    • Wednesday: Kwaku
    • Thurday: Yaw
    • Friday: Kofi
    • Saturday: Kwame
